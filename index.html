About Us

Leading Supplier of Office Furniture and Construction Materials.

Best Wing Solutions Ltd is one of the leading suppliers of high quality furniture and construction materials in East Africa. We are a pioneer in the industry offering the market greater choice, innovative and ergonomic designs as well as environmentally friendly furniture and construction materials. From office chairs to desks, ballast to flooring and roofing materials, we have expanded and grown over the years, allowing us to be more flexible to more areas of trade. Having established itself as a market leader for office furniture and construction materials, Best Wing Solutions Ltd. has also continued its growth strategy and has developed additional lines that includes Wardrobes, Joinery & Fit-Outs and Custom-Made Furniture. Our Services

Discover all our services...

Office Furniture

We offer a wide range of office systems and chairs that are flexible, dynamic and ergonomic that will help improve working Conditions. We understand your needs and requirements and we provide solutions to fulfill them.We have got you fully covered.



Space planning and Design

We Enhance your offices with professional space planning. Our dedicated design team will create 2D plans to illustrate to you the best use of your space. Taking inspiration from your needs and desires we will ensure that your new workplace provides a great place for your employees and clients to work.



Construction Supplies

We supply the best-in-class building and construction materials, equipment, tools and accessories. Whether you are working on a residential or commercial project, we've got you covered. Let us know what you need, and we will supply it. We always deliver to your site anywhere in Kenya within agreed timelines.



Delivery and installation

We understand the importance of the smooth running of every project from conception to completion. Our service is designed to offer our clients, complete peace of mind. Our installers are trained to the very highest standards, and have regular on-going training.



Project Management

We work with you to design, build and manage complex office furniture projects for you. We will handle your full project so as to manage the design and layout of your office space, the interior fit out, logistics and installation of your office furniture.
